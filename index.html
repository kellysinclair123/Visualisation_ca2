Car Tracking System

In Second year myself and fellow students were placed into groups to create a basic project involving a front-end and back-end system. we created a system that allows us to track cars we rent out by scanning each vehicle’s ID or tag. This made managing the rental process a lot more organized and helped us keep better control of our fleet as it informed us of points such as:

  • We can easily see where all the cars are at any given time.
  • It shows how many cars are in need of maintenance.
  • It keeps daily tracking simple and helps prevent double booking.
